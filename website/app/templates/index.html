Bringing Golf to Your Apartment

Many golfers live in urban areas where golf is not easily accessible, meaning we often have to hit balls into a net in a garage or basement.

While this is an inexpensive and time-saving option, the golfer doesn’t know where their shot would have gone. Without this knowledge, the quality of the practice is decreased and the experience is less enjoyable.

Currently, gaining this knowledge is very expensive, you have to somehow get a physical simulator.

With Azimuth, you can enjoy golf simply:

  • Only requires one ball and one club
  • No nets, no sensor, just you and you phone camera
  • Cutting-edge Machine Learning techniques and ancient Physics theories
  • Extremely affordable
  • Sharing results with your friends
